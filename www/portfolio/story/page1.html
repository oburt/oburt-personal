I was born and raised in the Pacific Northwest. If you're a local, I'm from Shoreline. If you're not, let's just call it Seattle.

Either way, if I ever say "pop" instead of "soda" or talk about the distinct differences between rain types, you know why.

I grew up playing soccer (13 years of it) and cycling (I've biked from Seattle to Portland in a weekend). I also come from a big family: three younger sisters, one older sister, and one older brother. I have four nieces and one nephew. And let's not get started on the aunts, uncles, and cousins. There's a lot of lessons from my childhood that I could share, but one stands out:

When we work together with people that we love, we can do amazing things.
And when that fails—well, at least we made some damn good memories.
