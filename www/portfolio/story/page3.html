Outside of class, I dedicated myself to two things while in college. The first: ultimate frisbee. A sport scorned by many and unknown to even more, ultimate became my new sport du jour (and perhaps du vie).

As a form of exercise, I am fascinated by its combination of endurance, explosiveness, and full-body strength.

As a game, I am thrilled by every huge skying catch, every diving layout d, and every 70-yard full-field huck.

And, as a community, I am forever thankful for the friends and teammates that I have had the honor of playing alongside.

The second: Fondu. Or, to be more precise, enjoyn.me turned SpotOn turned Fondu. It all started out of an entrepreneurship class at NYU that would change my life, the lives of Gauri, Nicky, Sarah, Tony, Xinyi, Mike, Kiryl, and hopefully the lives of many others...
