I was hired in 2009 as a development intern for a matchmaking startup called Brandhim. They were trying to make dating fun and simple.

Their proprietary matchmaking algorithm was based on research that indicated brand preferences are strong indicators in personal compatibility.

It was invaluable to gain experience with the Facebook platform at this time, but thank god they have worked more on documentation since then.

By the end of my internship, I had completed a working prototype with full matchmaking and messaging capabilities.
